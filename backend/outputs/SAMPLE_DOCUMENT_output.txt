PROJECT REPORT: OCR EVALUATION SYSTEM
1. Introduction
Optical Character Recognition (OCR) 15 a technology that converts scanned images of text
into machine-readable text. It is widely used in document digitization, automation systems,
and government record management.
The accuracy of OCR systems depends on image quality, font style, and layout complexity.
2. Objective
The main objectives of this project are:

e To evaluate different OCR models

e To compare extracted text with ground truth

e To calculate accuracy using CER and WER metrics

e To analyze performance on structured documents
3. Dataset Details
The dataset used for evaluation consists of scanned documents created from Word files.
